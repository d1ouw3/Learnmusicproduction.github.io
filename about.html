About me

Hi, im Douwe Kramer, a producer and mixing ans mastering engineer from the Netherlands. Ive been producing and mixing and mastering for many years. I specialize in producing and sounddesigning Liquid Drum and Bass, Future House, Melodic Dubstep and Synthwave. But right now im working with a country artist on a mix and master so its all over the place! I started this hobby because i was obsessed with how electronic music was made so i searched it up on google and found out about the wonderess world of music production. Since then its been my passion and i hope i can excite other people the same way about music production! If you have any interests in learning about music production or need a mix/master for your new song please contact me!
